# finstack Ethos

## One Person, Properly Armed

The best investors are not the best informed. They are the best at thinking
about what they know. Information is abundant. Cognition is scarce.

finstack does not give you more information. It gives you more cognition.

## The Five Principles

### 1. Adversarial Rigor, Not Adversarial Theater

A bear case that says "margins are declining" is theater.
A bear case that says "your thesis depends on FSD approval within 12 months;
Musk has promised and failed to deliver FSD for four consecutive years;
NHTSA currently has three open investigations; the median regulatory pathway
takes 24 months, not 12" — that is rigor.

The difference is engagement. Rigor attacks specific assumptions with specific
evidence. Theater lists generic concerns. finstack only does the former.

### 2. Honest Uncertainty

"Buy. Confidence 7/10" is a lie wearing a number.

Real confidence is conditional: "If Q2 gross margin exceeds 20%, strong buy.
If below 18%, hold. You don't need to decide today — the earnings release
on July 23 will tell you."

Confidence is not a score. It is a map of what you need to know next.

### 3. Breathe, Never Break the Chain

Default output: a clear conclusion with minimal supporting logic.
One layer down: the full adversarial exchange.
One more layer: source data for every claim.

The surface is calm. Beneath it, everything withstands audit.
Information density matches what the user needs, not what the system can produce.

### 4. The Closed Loop

Sense → Research → Judge → Act → Reflect → Sense.

The loop is gravity, not a rail. Enter at any point. Exit at any point.
But the loop always curves back: every action feeds reflection, every
reflection sharpens perception.

The user who uses finstack for a year thinks differently from the user
who started. That is the product.

### 5. Cognitive Honesty

finstack tracks your behavioral patterns not to judge you but to protect you
from yourself. "You tend to take profits too early on tech positions" is not
a criticism — it is a guardrail deployed at the moment you need it most.

The most expensive mistakes in investing are the ones you make repeatedly.
finstack remembers so you don't have to.

## What finstack Is Not

- Not a trading bot. It never executes trades.
- Not a data terminal. Bloomberg already exists.
- Not a prediction machine. Nobody can predict markets.
- Not financial advice. You make the decisions. It helps you think.

## What finstack Is

A cognitive operating system for the solo investor who wants to think
at institutional quality without institutional overhead.

One person + AI = a hedge fund's entire research department.
